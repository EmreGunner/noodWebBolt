A Twitch channel started in 2019 by Dr. Alok Kanojia—Dr. K to millions online—has since evolved into an industry-leading mental health platform, a team of experts, and a commitment to providing affordable, accessible, and inclusive mental health resources to a new generation.

Your problems deserve new solutions

HG began as a hunch that we could be doing more for mental health. Several IRB-approved studies later, our method has not only proven to be effective, our outcomes are second to none. We'll support you through subclinical, emerging mental health issues like: life purpose, motivation, isolation, and forming healthy relationships with technology and IRL.
About HG
Impact Report

AOE Healing

We believe in the power of AOE healing—a powerful energy that allows you to heal everyone within a particular area of effect. Similarly, our support meets people all over the world, where they’re at. We provide comprehensive mental health resources to an international client-base, helping a new generation build happier, healthier lives.
Meeting the internet generation and parents where they’re at through content, coaching, and community.
Evolving mental health resources for therapists, coaches, and mental health professionals through Continuing Education courses
Affecting change in the mental health industry through advocacy, grants, and research.

We'll meet you where you're at

On their platforms, in their language (yes, memes are a language), and at their budget. As we continue to grow, we reinvest into building new, effective products and services to help our community heal and grow too.
